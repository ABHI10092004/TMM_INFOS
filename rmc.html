Ready Mix Concrete (RMC)

Fresh, high-quality concrete delivered right to your construction site

About Ready Mix Concrete

Our Ready Mix Concrete (RMC) is manufactured in a controlled environment using precise proportions of cement, aggregates, water, and admixtures. Delivered fresh to your site in specialized transit mixers for immediate use.

Consistent quality and strength
Precise mix proportions
Timely delivery to site
Reduced construction time

RMC Grades Available

Choose the right grade for your construction needs

M15

15 N/mm²

Suitable for non-structural work

  • Plain concrete work
  • Leveling courses
  • Non-load bearing walls

M20

20 N/mm²

Standard grade for residential construction

  • Residential foundations
  • Slabs and beams
  • Columns up to 2 floors

M25

25 N/mm²

High-strength concrete for commercial use

  • Commercial buildings
  • Multi-story structures
  • Heavy load applications

M30+

30+ N/mm²

High-performance concrete for special applications

  • Industrial structures
  • Bridges and highways
  • High-rise buildings
