How We Make It

State-of-the-art manufacturing processes ensure superior quality

M-Sand Supply

At TMM INFRA, we source high-quality manufactured sand (M-Sand) only from trusted and certified vendors. Our vendor partnerships ensure that every batch meets construction standards and supports durability in projects.

  • Sourcing from reputed and certified crushing units
  • Washed and well-graded sand suitable for all construction needs
  • Vendor-tested quality
  • Proper storage and safe handling before delivery

Manufacturing Blocks

Our concrete blocks are manufactured using automated machinery and premium materials. Our process ensures:

  • Precise mixing of cement, sand, and aggregates
  • Reduced mortar usage and faster wall construction saves overall project cost.
  • Hydraulic pressing for uniform density
  • Steam curing for optimal strength
  • Quality control at every stage
