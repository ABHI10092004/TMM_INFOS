Jelly 20mm

Standard-sized aggregate for robust concrete construction

About Jelly 20mm

Our 20mm Jelly is a standard-sized aggregate widely used in general construction for its excellent strength and durability. It's the perfect choice for most concrete applications requiring robust performance.

Uniform 20mm size for optimal strength
Ideal for standard concrete mixes
Superior durability and load-bearing capacity
Consistent quality for reliable performance
