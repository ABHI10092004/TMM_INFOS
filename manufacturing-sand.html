Manufacturing Sand Process

Advanced technology and quality control for premium sand production

Our Sand Manufacturing Process

From raw materials to premium quality sand

Raw Material Selection

High-quality granite and quartzite rocks are carefully selected from our quarries

Primary Crushing

Large rocks are crushed into smaller pieces using jaw crushers

Secondary Crushing

Further crushing with cone crushers to achieve desired particle size

Screening & Washing

Multi-deck screens separate different sizes, followed by thorough washing

Quality Testing

Rigorous testing for particle size, silt content, and other quality parameters

Storage & Dispatch

Proper storage in covered areas and efficient dispatch to customers

Advanced Technology
