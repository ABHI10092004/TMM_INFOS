Jelly Aggregates

Premium quality aggregates in various sizes for all your construction needs

About Jelly Aggregates

Our Jelly Aggregates are premium quality crushed stone aggregates available in various sizes (6mm, 12mm, 20mm, and 40mm) to meet different construction requirements. These aggregates are essential components in concrete production, providing strength, stability, and durability to structures.

Multiple size options for various applications
High compressive strength
Excellent durability and load-bearing capacity
Consistent quality and grading
View Process

Jelly Types

Explore our range of jelly aggregate sizes

Jelly 6mm

Our 6mm Jelly is the finest aggregate in our range, perfect for thin concrete sections, repair works, and finishing applications where a smooth surface is required.

Particle Size

5mm - 7mm

Bulk Density

1450-1550 kg/m³

Applications

  • Thin concrete overlays
  • Repair and patching work
  • Precast concrete products
  • Decorative concrete finishes

Jelly 12mm

Our 12mm Jelly is a versatile medium-sized aggregate suitable for general concrete work, providing excellent workability and strength for various construction applications.

Particle Size

10mm - 14mm

Bulk Density

1500-1600 kg/m³

Applications

  • General concrete work
  • Reinforced concrete structures
  • Driveways and pathways
  • Small to medium construction projects

Jelly 20mm

Our 20mm Jelly is a standard-sized aggregate widely used in general construction for its excellent strength and durability. It's the perfect choice for most concrete applications requiring robust performance.

Particle Size

16mm - 22mm

Bulk Density

1550-1650 kg/m³

Applications

  • Standard concrete mixes
  • Structural concrete work
  • Foundations and footings
  • Commercial and residential construction

Jelly 40mm

Our 40mm Jelly is our largest aggregate size, ideal for heavy-duty construction projects, mass concrete works, and applications requiring maximum strength and stability.

Particle Size

35mm - 45mm

Bulk Density

1600-1700 kg/m³

Applications

  • Mass concrete works
  • Heavy-duty foundations
  • Road base construction
  • Large infrastructure projects
